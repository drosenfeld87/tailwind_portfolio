Who is this person?

You've made it this far and are probably expecting an explanation

I've been desigining in a wide range of roles for the past 15 years. I started with photography and photo editing, then moved into graphic design for print, then digital marketing design. In 2017 I realized I wanted to move away from print and digital design to become more web-focused. So I did a coding bootcamp, then got a job doing web design form my experience there. While working there, I saw I needed to get a better understanding of UI/UX in order to create better websites and apps, so I got funding to enroll in a part-time UI/UX bootcamp. In late 2019 I landed my current job as a product designer, which is the space I'm growing my career in. I live in Toronto, Canada, which is the place I call home.
