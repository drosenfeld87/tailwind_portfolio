Exponential IT Readiness Diagnostic Report

Overview

The Exponential IT Readiness Diagnostic Report is a framework defined by Info-Tech Research Group to advise IT leaders on how to elevate their maturity and close the gap between the exponential progression of technological change, and  IT's ability to successfully manage that change.​ This report is generated from the survey results taken by IT leaders and their counterparts.
