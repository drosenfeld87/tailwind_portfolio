Goals

The goal of the project is to design a report based on a survey assessing an organization's readiness for exponential technologies. As a designer, the focus is on creating a report that provides insights into both organizational and IT readiness for transformation. The report highlights opportunities, risks, and stakeholder insights, while also assessing IT leaders' confidence in supporting transformation. It helps stakeholders understand the need for change, set priorities, and track progress annually through benchmarking, ultimately supporting IT teams and leadership in adopting exponential IT.
