Customer Case Studies

Overview

The Customer Case Studies project was designed to showcase real-world client success stories, building credibility and demonstrating the value of Info-Tech’s solutions. This initiative included creating a dedicated website section with a user-friendly homepage, detailed individual case study pages, and a CMS component enabling the marketing team to manage content independently. By enhancing engagement, streamlining workflows, and reinforcing Info-Tech’s brand positioning, the project has become a vital tool for driving trust and showcasing the company’s impact.

My role

  • Product Designer
  • UI/UX Designer
  • Front end developer

Year

  • 2023-2024

Company

  • Info-Tech Research Group

Situation

Info-Tech Research Group needed a dedicated online section to highlight customer success stories and demonstrate the value of its services. The existing website lacked a centralized location for these case studies, and the marketing team had limited ability to manage content independently.

Task

I was tasked with designing a comprehensive Customer Case Studies section. This included creating a homepage to list all case studies, designing individual pages for detailed stories, and developing a CMS component that empowered the marketing team to create and manage case studies without developer assistance.

Action

To address these needs, I:

  1. Collaborated with marketing and content teams to identify requirements and establish a clear vision for the section.
  2. Designed a user-friendly homepage featuring a dynamic grid layout with filtering options for easy navigation.
  3. Crafted individual case study pages with structured layouts emphasizing client challenges, solutions, and outcomes, supported by visual elements like logos and metrics.
  4. Developed a reusable CMS component with pre-set styles and drag-and-drop functionality to streamline content creation for non-technical users.
  5. Ensured the entire section was responsive and aligned with Info-Tech’s brand guidelines.

Result

The new Customer Case Studies section significantly enhanced Info-Tech’s ability to share client success stories effectively. The intuitive CMS component reduced dependency on developers, enabling the marketing team to independently create and manage content. The improved design has also made it easier for potential clients to explore relevant case studies, bolstering Info-Tech's credibility and driving engagement.
